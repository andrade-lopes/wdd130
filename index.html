Cape Verde

Cape Verde is a country in the western Africa. Cape Verde or Cabo Verde, officially the Republic of Cabo Verde, is an island country and archipelagic state of West Africa in the central Atlantic Ocean, consisting of ten volcanic islands with a combined land area of about 4,033 square kilometers (1,557 sq mi). These islands lie between 600 and 850 kilometers (320 and 460 nautical miles) west of Cap-Vert, the westernmost point of continental Africa.
